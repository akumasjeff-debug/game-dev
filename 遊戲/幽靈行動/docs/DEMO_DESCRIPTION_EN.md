## Full Description

---

### You don't fight. You command.

A four-member special ops team infiltrates a hostile facility. They move automatically, engage enemies on their own, and pull fallen teammates out of harm's way — all without you lifting a finger.

Your only job: **make the right call when it matters.**

A reinforced door blocks the path. Do you have the Shield Operator smash through it, or wait for the Demolitions Expert to plant charges? Intel suggests an ambush ahead. Press forward, or neutralize it with a smoke grenade first? Every decision ripples forward. Some mistakes cost HP. Others cost the whole mission.

---

### Core Gameplay

**Idle Progression x Decision Interruption x Card Progression**

- Your squad auto-advances through every mission. Put the phone down — your base keeps generating gold while you're away.
- When a decision point triggers, the action pauses. You must intervene. Choose right, and your squad pushes forward. Choose wrong, and you'll feel it.
- Each of the 6 character classes can be upgraded to Level 9. Higher-level characters unlock hidden mission events that only trigger if you brought the right person — the same mission plays out differently depending on who's on your team.
- Failure has no penalty. Your base keeps running. Retry immediately with the same squad, or swap out members and try a different approach.

---

### Six Classes. Four Slots. Infinite Combinations.

| Class | Role |
|-------|------|
| Shield Operator | Front-line tank. At Lv.9, can bash through reinforced doors without explosives. |
| Assault | Primary damage output. Fastest attack speed. Best for rapid room clearing. |
| Demolitions | AOE burst damage. Destroys cover. Can chain-detonate three rooms simultaneously at Lv.9. |
| Medic | Auto-heals the most injured teammate. At Lv.9, can revive a fallen squad member mid-mission. |
| Sniper | Long-range single-target elimination. Ideal for neutralizing Boss escorts before engagement. |
| Recon | Reveals enemy count in unknown rooms before entry. Information is your most powerful weapon. |

You bring 4 of these 6 into every mission. Different compositions unlock different decision options and trigger different hidden events. A team without a Recon operator goes in blind. A team without a Medic plays without a safety net.
